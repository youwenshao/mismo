# Client Guide: What to Expect

This document outlines the step-by-step process of a Mismo project, from initial discovery to final delivery.

---

## 🗓️ Project Timeline

Mismo projects typically follow a 5-stage lifecycle.

### Stage 1: Discovery (1-3 Days)
- **PRD Discovery**: Our agents interview you to refine your product requirements.
- **Feasibility Score**: We assess the project's complexity and safety.
- **PRD Review**: You review and lock the final PRD.

### Stage 2: Contracted & Pre-flight (1 Day)
- **Payment**: Initial platform fee is processed.
- **Contract Signed**: Intellectual Property (IP) and data boundary agreements are executed.
- **Build Initialization**: We configure your project's workspace and select the appropriate agent swarm.

### Stage 3: Development (3-7 Days)
- **Agent Swarm**: Our database, backend, and frontend agents build your product in parallel.
- **Status Updates**: You receive automated email/Slack updates as each milestone is reached.
- **Human Review**: Our engineers periodically review agent output to ensure quality.

### Stage 4: Verification & QA (1-2 Days)
- **Contract Verification**: We verify the build against the PRD.
- **QA Agent**: Automated tests are run to ensure functionality and security.
- **Walkthrough Video**: An automated walkthrough of the deployed app is recorded.

### Stage 5: Delivery (1 Day)
- **Code Handoff**: The project is pushed to your GitHub repository.
- **Hosting Transfer**: We help you deploy the app to your hosting provider (Vercel, AWS, etc.).
- **Ownership Transfer**: You are invited as an admin to the repository and hosting account.

---

## 📦 What You'll Receive

Every Mismo project includes the following deliverables:

1.  **Source Code**: Full access to the GitHub repository with clean history.
2.  **Architecture Decision Record (ADR)**: Documentation of key technical decisions.
3.  **API & Data Contracts**: Formal specifications of the system's interfaces.
4.  **Operational Runbook**: A guide for maintaining and modifying the system.
5.  **Walkthrough Video**: An automated recording of the app's key features.
6.  **Hosting Contract**: A summary of the hosting SLAs, backup plans, and scaling limits.

---

## 🤝 Your Role in the Process

To ensure a successful delivery, we ask for your help in:

- **Providing Clear Requirements**: The more detailed your PRD, the better the agents perform.
- **Timely Feedback**: Reviewing PRDs and contract drafts helps maintain the project timeline.
- **Accepting Invites**: Accepting the GitHub collaborator invite within 24 hours is critical for the delivery pipeline.
